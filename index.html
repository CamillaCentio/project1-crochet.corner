Welcome to Crochet Corner

Who are we?

We are still a small group of crocheters. We hope to grow and expand our group over time. We started this page during the pandemic, a time where we can't meet outside, we took a very difficult time and made it a little easier. We have one thing in common and that is that we love to crochet.

What do we do?

We will have meetings over Google Meet. You can sit wherever you want, whatever time of day you want and you can still wear your pajamas. During a meeting we talk about everything, life, crochet, we drink coffee or tea and of course we crochet together. Sometimes not everyone in our small community can join a meeting, but I can guarantee you that there will always be someone there, you will never be alone.
